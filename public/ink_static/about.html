About

About Me

In 2007, I received my Bachelor’s of Science in Electrical Engineering from California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo.

After graduation, I worked as a High School Youth Pastor at FPCSLO for 3 years where I was able to spend time with teenage boys and girls and take groups of kids to serve the poor in Guatemala, the Dominican Republic, and Las Vegas.

In 2011, my beautiful wife Laura and I moved to Norway where I pursued (and eventually received) my Master’s Degree in Computer Science from the University of Agder.

Work

I’ve worked for companies like VMWare, MyHeritage, and Geni. I’ve created app/plugins/projects like FontPrep, jquery.pep.js, Sirkel (featured on The Next Web), Compass2CSS, among others.
